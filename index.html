VisionStatement

To build a healthy community where everyone is taken care of, physically, socially, emotionally, and economically. Read More

ObjectiveStatement

To render Home based care services to residents of soweto from Mondays to fridays – To transfer skills on Home based Care services to family member of a sick person - To offer after school services to orphans and valnerable children - To network with other like-minded institutions - To establish skills development programs in vegetable farming.Read More

What weDo

Apart from taking care of bedridden patients we also run a feeding program which currently caters for up to 250 children, providing meals twice a week at our center - We also run an after school programs for the children in our community - We do skills development for the youth - We support 50 elderly persons with medical supplies and food packs - We also have a food garden and produce, feed the community memebers - We also support families in distress with food parcels and clothing from the donations we receive.:Home,Our Mission,News and Press,How to Help,Contact Us.Read More

Our Mission

Providing and promoting comprehensive health care services to people infected by chronic diseases and disabilities as well as giving support and counseling to the affected families.

Who We Are

Soweto Home Based Care was established in 2002 in response to the high in demand for care workers who were assigned to take care of bedridden patients within our community. Our care workers are qualified and acquired the much needed skills to perform their tasks

When

Mondays and Wednesdays are dedicated to our patients while Tuesday and Thursdays we run the feeding programs. Fridays are dedicated to fundraising. We get most of our groceries from individual sponsors even though the sponsorships are not consistent. We also have a garden on our premises where we get most of our vegetables from.
